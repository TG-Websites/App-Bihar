Aam Aadmi Party Bihar

The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) is a political party committed to true politics and transparent governance in India. In Bihar, our goal is to provide better education, healthcare, and corruption-free governance to the people of the state.

We believe that public participation in politics is essential. Our party was formed for those who believe in honest politics and want to work for the betterment of society.

Our Principles

  • Transparent and honest governance
  • Quality education for all
  • Free and better healthcare
  • Continuous fight against corruption
  • Direct public participation
